CourseRate

Discover your next course.

Make informed decisions on course selections and expectations with insights from experienced alumni.

It's like Reddit, but for your courses.

Here's how it works:
  1. Experienced alumni review a course and offer advice for future students
  2. Students at your school upvote and downvote existing reviews
  3. Anyone can read the top reviews for any course, curated by their peers

You pick the best reviews.

Students upvote and downvote existing reviews based on quality. The best-rated reviews rise to the top.

We prioritize quality over controversy. Downvotes are considered as serious expressions of dissatisfaction and are much more detrimental to review rankings than upvotes are beneficial to them.

All reviews stay anonymous.

Review your courses without worrying about your identity. Let's focus on the courses, not the authors.

Bring it to your school!

CourseRate is flexible enough to support any school with courses. All we need is your school's email domain and a table of courses. To add your school's courses to CourseRate, let us know.
